Recommender System

I developed a recommender system to predict user interests and suggest relevant products or friends. The project explored various algorithms, including collaborative filtering, which recommends mutual friends; influence scoring, which ranks friends based on their connections; and content-based filtering, which suggests items based on a user's previous preferences. After comparing these approaches, I concluded that combining influence scoring with content-based filtering provides the most accurate recommendations by considering both user preferences and the strength of connections.
